Message from the President

ACCURATE AND TIMELY DIAGNOSIS IS KEY TO CURE HUMAN AILMENTS

Undeveloped countries lack adequate medical equipment for proper diagnosis. Japan has high quality and precise diagnostic equipment, and many instruments are replaced by newer systems while still useful.

Fair Medical was established to connect hospitals, doctors, dealers and overseas medical teams through reliable purchase and sales of used medical equipment. We provide prompt response, fair pricing, shipping coordination and ongoing customer support.

We provide sincere support to clients from major manufacturers of MRI, CT scanner, ultrasound, X-ray and endoscopy systems.
