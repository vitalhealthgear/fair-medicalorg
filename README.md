Contact form setup
------------------
The Contact Us page uses FormSubmit to route submissions to admin@fair-medical.org and send an automatic acknowledgement to the sender.

Important: after publishing, submit the contact form once and open the confirmation email sent to admin@fair-medical.org. Click the confirmation link. After that, form messages will deliver normally.

The form redirects successful submissions to thank-you.html.
